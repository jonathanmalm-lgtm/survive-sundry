Your alarm goes off at 6:00. You have seventeen unread emails from last night. You read them before getting out of bed. Three require responses before 8 AM. You draft two of them in your head while making breakfast. Someone asks what you're thinking about. "Nothing," you say. "Catering invoices," you say, two seconds later.

Sunday is technically not your domain. Sunday is pastoral. Sunday is creative. Sunday is the front of the house and you are the back. But the back runs the front, and everyone knows it, and Pastor Dave knows it, and that is why you are going in at 7:00 AM on your day off because the folding chair count for Service 2 is twelve short and nobody else noticed.

Welcome to Execution Church. Today is Sunday. This Sunday could make or break everything.
