Twenty minutes after second service. Everyone is standing. Nobody has eaten since the huddle donuts four hours ago. This is not a meeting with food. This is a meeting where you find out what people actually thought.

Campus pastor goes first. Win — both services ran. Miss — he missed a check-in window with the team during turnover. He looks around.

Before your turn Gary the head usher says: 'I just want to be honest, I have a few facilities observations.' Gary has been making facilities observations since 7:15 AM and several of them have made their way to your list. You look at Gary. Gary looks at you. The campus pastor looks at both of you. The room senses something is happening between you and Gary.

## A

The campus pastor writes things down. When you get to the database error he stops writing. 'When did you catch that?' he asks. 'During setup,' you say. 'And it's fixed?' 'Clean by turnover,' you say. He writes something. He looks at Gary. 'Your observations are noted. Send them to her directly.' Gary nods. Gary will send them. Gary will CC himself.

## B

Clean. Specific. The campus pastor nods at the facility win. He asks one follow-up question about the database miss and accepts the answer. Gary's observations go unaddressed in this meeting. They will arrive in your inbox before 5:00 PM. Gary CCs himself.

## C

The campus pastor accepts this. Later in the week he hears about the database issue from the tech leader. He hears about the calendar conflict from the elder board. He circles back to you Friday. The conversation is professional and not unkind. The missed opportunity to name the issues early is present in the room without being named.

## D:win

Gary receives the floor. Gary was not expecting the floor. Gary has been here eleven years. Gary says: 'I made some adjustments to improve flow.' You say: 'I documented those adjustments.' Gary looks at you. The campus pastor says: 'Let's align on a process for physical changes. Both of you.' Gary nods. You nod. This is the most productive thing that has happened between you and Gary all day.

## D:lose

Gary receives the floor and immediately produces a counter-list. The counter-list has seventeen items. Three of them are from 2022. The campus pastor receives the counter-list with the patience of a man who has been managing Gary for longer than you have been working here. 'Let's table this,' he says. The meeting moves on. Gary has not finished his list. Gary knows the meeting moved on. Gary will continue the list via email.

Around the room: Tech reports clean. Worship says second service connected. Kids ministry describes Jackson in one sentence. First Impressions says the door felt warm but the lobby felt cold. Nobody claims the lobby. Someone says the haze was a lot today. Nobody claims the haze.

'Same time next week.'

Four seconds.

Gary moves first.
