You're offstage again. You are hiding near the back hallway with a granola bar when Chad finds you. Chad is a bartender at Chili's but he's very into finance. Chad has been giving you unsolicited financial advice since October.

'Have you heard of BlippiCoin?'

It is a cryptocurrency. It is based on Blippi. The children's entertainer. Chad says this with the same energy and confidence he would use to describe a blue chip stock. You are holding a granola bar. The sermon is happening fifteen feet away. Chad has a deck.

## A

Chad respects the boundary. He finds the campus pastor instead. Campus pastor has questions for you later about 'a guy named Chad.'

## B

3 minutes becomes 11. You miss something happening inside the service. You don't know what yet. You also now know more about BlippiCoin than any worship leader in history.

## C

Chad sends a 14-paragraph email at 11:45 PM with the subject line: 'THE FUTURE — for your eyes only.' You will never respond. Chad will follow up twice.

## D:win

You have invested $200 in a cryptocurrency backed by a man in an orange and blue outfit who teaches children about excavators. You did this during a sermon. You did this hiding near a granola bar. Somewhere Blippi is completely unaware that he funded a subwoofer for a church in your city. Chad finds out about the $124,000. Chad texts you seventeen times in one day. You do not respond to Chad. Chad is still a bartender at Chili's.

## D:lose

BlippiCoin is worth $0. You tell no one. You will tell everyone eventually. The story gets funnier every time. The $200 does not come back.
