You're offstage again. You are hiding near the back hallway with a granola bar when Chad finds you. Chad is a bartender at Chili's but he's very into finance. Chad has been giving you unsolicited financial advice since October.

'Have you heard of BlippiCoin?'

It's a cryptocurrency. It's based on Blippi. The children's entertainer. Chad's convinced this crypto is going to increase in value like crazy. You're chewing your granola bar.

Chad's pushy. He thinks you should invest.

## A

Chad respects the boundary. You were just one stop on his journey working through the staff. His heart is in the right place, but you're hoping he chills out soon.

## B

3 minutes becomes 11. You're rather entertained by his pitch, if only for the sheer ridiculousness of it. When the congregation applauds in the worship center, you realize you missed something. Hopefully nothing important. You excuse yourself from Chad content in the knowledge that you now know more about BlippiCoin than any worship leader in history.

## C

Chad doesn't send you an informational email. He sends you links to four separate 45-minute YouTube videos. You watch two minutes of the first one before you zone out. When Chad asks you what you think next week you fake your way through the conversation and say you're seriously considering buying in. Lying isn't a sin if it's about crypto.
