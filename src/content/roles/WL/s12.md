Twenty minutes after second service. Everyone is standing. Nobody has eaten since the huddle donuts four hours ago. This is not a meeting with food. This is a meeting where you find out what people actually thought.

Campus pastor goes first. Win — both services ran. Miss — he was in the lobby talking to people during turnover instead of checking in with the team. He looks at you.

Before you can answer Gary the head usher says: 'I just want to be honest, I thought worship was a struggle today.' Gary says this with the gentle sincerity of a man who believes he is being helpful.

## A

The room is quiet. Pastor: 'What do you need from me for next week?' This is the question you needed. Gary nods like he knew it all along. You do not look at Gary.

## B

Clean. Greg says: 'Second service had something the first didn't. What changed?' Gary says 'I noticed that too' about something he wasn't part of. Gary is now part of it.

## C

Nobody asks considering what. Gary opens his mouth. The campus pastor raises his hand slightly in Gary's direction. Gary closes his mouth. You file this away.

## D:win

Gary did not set up the chairs differently. The chairs are exactly the same as every week. Gary knows this. You know this. The room is not sure. Gary opens his mouth. Gary closes his mouth. Gary is now thinking about the chairs. Gary spends the rest of the meeting visibly trying to remember if he set up the chairs differently. He did not. Next week Gary arrives 30 minutes early to make absolutely sure the chairs are right. This is the only good outcome of what you just did and you know it.

## D:lose

Gary did not set up the chairs differently. The campus pastor finds you after the meeting. He does not raise his voice. He says: 'Did you just gaslight Gary about the chairs?' You say: 'I raised a concern about the setup.' He looks at you for a moment. 'Same time next week,' he says. It is not a warm same time next week.

Around the room: Tech leader reports the stream was clean. Kids ministry says Jackson is coming back next week. First Impressions says the door felt warm but the lobby felt cold. Nobody claims the lobby. Someone says the haze was a lot today. Nobody claims the haze.
