Services are done. A mom stops you. Her son Marcus is 15. 'He wants to learn how to play guitar and I was hoping you could teach him. Maybe mentor him? Give him a place to play?'

It's clear Marcus' mom wants him to learn guitar. Not Marcus. If a teenager really wants to learn guitar YouTube can teach them faster than you can.

But Marcus' mom is persistent. Based on how miserable Marcus looks to be in church, you know there's probably more behind it than just learning guitar.

## A

Marcus shows up. He learns quickly. Even has a decent strum pattern.

At the end you both are much more interested in the arrangement. You invite him back next week and he seems excited.

## B

Marcus' mom is very disappointed. She thought you would take more interest in the church of tomorrow. Marcus looks grateful to get to look back at his phone and slink away.

## C

Marcus perks up, if for no other reason than you kind of dissed his mom. 'What do you think, Marcus. Do you want to learn guitar?'

Marcus looks at his mom's scowl. He looks back to you. 'Yeah. Actually that would be pretty cool.'

You invite him to rehearsal. 'See what it's like to be in a band, and then I'll show you some chords after rehearsal. We'll see how you like it.'

'Bet.'

They walk away and Marcus's mom looks back at you with a softened expression. 'Thank you,' she mouths.

## D:win

'Looking to rizz up some huzzes with your axe skills?' you say. Immediate cringe. You aren't even sure what that means.

'Eh, just joking,' you say, hoping Marcus buys it.

Marcus laughs. 'Yeah. Exactly.'

Marcus' mom is pleasantly surprised. She hasn't heard Marcus' laugh in months.

You tell him to watch a few YouTube videos and check in with you next week.

'Bet,' he says. 'Bet," his mom agrees.

## D:lose

'Looking to rizz up some huzzes with your axe skills?' you say. Immediate cringe. You aren't even sure what that means.

Marcus' mom looks at you like you're having a stroke. Marcus says one word: 'No.' His look makes you want to crawl into a tomb and bury yourself.

'Here. Watch these YouTube videos and get back to me.' You scurry away as fast as you can and vow to never talk to another teenager again.
