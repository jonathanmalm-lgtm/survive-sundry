Your alarm goes off at 6:30 AM. You pick up your phone to turn it off and accidentally open Instagram. You tell yourself you'll put it down in thirty seconds. You put it down eleven minutes later. You do not know what you looked at. It is already gone. You get up.

Your wallpaper is a photo of yourself leading worship on Christmas Eve two years ago. The room was packed. The lights were perfect. Something happened that night that you have been trying to recreate on a random Sunday in March ever since. It has not happened again. You are aware this is not a healthy relationship with a wallpaper. You have not changed it.

Welcome to Execution Church. Today is Sunday. This Sunday could make or break everything.
