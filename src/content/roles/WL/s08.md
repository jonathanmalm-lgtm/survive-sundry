Nineteen minutes between services. Band needs a reset, Cody needs reassurance, the stage has a water bottle someone left that will be visible on camera, and you still haven't eaten anything except that one iced coffee which was a mistake in more ways than one.

Your phone rings. It's your mom. She does not know you're at work. In her mind, church is a place you go, not a place you work. She wants to tell you about an article she saw on Facebook. Brandon Lake apparently sat down with President Donald Trump to discuss the current state of worship in America. She has several thoughts about what Brandon Lake said. The article is AI generated. You know this immediately. You have told her how to spot AI generated content four times. It never sticks.

## A

She leaves a 3-minute voicemail. The last 40 seconds are her trying to figure out how to hang up. You will listen to all of it.

## B

It goes 7 minutes. You miss the band reset huddle. Cody made a decision without you. It was a bold decision.

## C

She responds with 4 prayer hands emojis and 'praying for your ministry baby.' You feel genuinely loved and vaguely embarrassed at the same time. You reset the stage in full focus. You are ready for second service.

## D:win

Your mom's voice in the lobby during reset is oddly grounding. Cody hears her ask 'is that your mother?' and respects you slightly less and slightly more simultaneously. You finish the reset. You are mostly ready for second service.

## D:lose

Your mom hears the chaos. She asks if everything is okay. She asks if you're eating. She reads you another Brandon Lake quote. Then she starts praying out loud on the phone in the middle of the lobby reset. She prays specifically that God would give the nation's worship leaders wisdom in these political times, referencing the Brandon Lake article as evidence that this is already happening. A first-time guest hears this and assumes it's normal. You reset the stage with one hand and hold the phone with the other and somewhere Brandon Lake is completely unaware that any of this is happening.
