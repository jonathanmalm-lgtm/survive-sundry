Twenty minutes after second service. Everyone is standing. Nobody has eaten since the huddle donuts four hours ago. This is not a meeting with food. This is a meeting where you find out what people actually thought.

Campus pastor goes first. Win — both services. Miss — he was in the lobby during turnover and not with the team. He looks at you.

Before you can answer Gary the head usher says: 'I just want to be honest, I thought the lobby had some gaps today.' Gary was in the lobby for parts of today. Gary is not wrong about the gaps. Gary is also not the reason for the gaps. You nod slowly.

## A

The room listens. When you get to K. Morris the campus pastor writes something. When you get to the family who came back, he looks up. 'Tell me more about that,' he says. You do. That's the last thing on your report. The meeting ends on that. It's the right note.

## B

Clean. The campus pastor accepts the overflow call. He doesn't ask about K. Morris. Someone else will find the review eventually. You have a plan for it. That's enough for today.

## C

Nobody asks. The campus pastor writes 'door/lobby' in his notes. You have given him the shape of a problem without the substance. He will ask you on Monday. You will have to reconstruct it from memory. Some of the detail will be gone by Monday.

## D:win

The room goes quiet when you name the review. The campus pastor looks at you directly. 'Two stars,' he says. 'Two stars,' you confirm. The team processes this together. It is the most honest the room has been all day. Gary says: 'I've been here eleven years and we've always had good reviews.' The campus pastor writes something. You do not ask what.

## D:lose

The room goes quieter than you expected. Tech team checks their phones. Worship team checks their phones. Someone Googles the church. Someone reads the review out loud without meaning to read it out loud. The campus pastor says: 'Let's address this offline.' Gary says: 'I just want to be honest, that door does close slowly.' Nobody responds to Gary.

Around the room: Tech reports the stream was clean. Worship says second service had something. Kids ministry says Jackson is coming back next week. That's the win and the miss. Nobody claims the lobby when it comes up. Someone says the haze was a lot today. Nobody claims the haze.

'Same time next week.'

Four seconds.

Gary moves first.
