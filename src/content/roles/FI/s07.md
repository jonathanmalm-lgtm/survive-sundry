You're in the lobby during the sermon. This is normal. The lobby is your second service.

Ron finds you. Eleven years at Execution Church. Ron has opinions about the door. Specifically, the front door. The door opens too easily, according to Ron. People come in after service has started and they come in loud. The door mechanism should have a slower close. Also the greeters at the secondary entrance have, in Ron's observation, inconsistent energy. Some Sundays they are warm. Some Sundays they are 'just standing there.' Ron has been tracking this.

Ron tells you what he tithes each month. It is your entire salary. From one person. Ron would like you to know that. Ron wants a response.
