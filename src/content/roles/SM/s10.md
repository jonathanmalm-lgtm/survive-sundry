After second service. You're in the lobby editing content on your phone. You have three strong shots from this morning and twelve mediocre ones and you're making decisions.

Chad finds you. Chad is a bartender at Chili's but he's very into finance. Chad has been making his way through the staff all morning. He found you specifically because — and he says this with complete sincerity — 'you work in media and this is a media opportunity.' Chad has a deck. The deck is about BlippiCoin — a cryptocurrency backed by Blippi, the children's entertainer.

You spend six hours a week looking at what's performing in kids content. Blippi is everywhere. Blippi has 26 million YouTube subscribers. Chad is not wrong that this is a content presence.

Chad is wrong about almost everything else.

## A

'I appreciate it but no.' Chad retreats. You go back to editing. You finish with three strong posts scheduled for the week. Blippi is fine. Blippi will continue to be fine without your $200.

## B

You give him five minutes. The five minutes become nine. One of your scheduled posts goes up without a final review because you were distracted. The post has a small but noticeable typo. Ron notices the typo. Ron emails you about the typo. The email is thorough.

## C

Chad emails you the deck at 11:52 PM. The deck's font is called 'Digital Rave.' You forward it to the kids ministry leader because you know she will appreciate the font. She responds: 'DIGITAL RAVE' with no other context. This is enough.

## D:win

You have invested $200 in a cryptocurrency backed by a man in an orange and blue outfit who teaches children about excavators. You understand content. You understand audiences. You invested in Blippi. BlippiCoin is worth $124,000 by the month-later meeting. You consider making this a case study. You decide not to make this a case study. You tithe it. You never tell anyone where the tithe came from. Blippi is completely unaware.

## D:lose

You have invested $200 in a cryptocurrency backed by a man in an orange and blue outfit who teaches children about excavators. You work in media. You understand content. You know better than this. BlippiCoin is worth $0. You tell no one. Every Tuesday when you sit down to edit the week's content you think about BlippiCoin for exactly four seconds. Then you get back to work.
