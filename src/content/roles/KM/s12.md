Twenty minutes after second service. Everyone is standing. Nobody has eaten since the huddle donuts four hours ago. This is not a meeting with food. This is a meeting where you find out what people actually thought.

Campus pastor goes first. Win and miss. He looks around. It comes to you.

Before you can speak Gary the head usher says: 'I just want to be honest, I heard it was a little loud in the kids wing today.' Gary was not in the kids wing. Gary is never in the kids wing. Gary heard something from someone, or Gary heard something from no one, and Gary has decided that this is the time to mention it.

## A

The room listens. When you get to the allergy situation the campus pastor writes something. When you get to Jackson he nods. When you're done he says: 'What do you need from me?' You tell him. He writes it down. He actually writes it down.

## B

The room receives this cleanly. Nobody questions the win. Nobody questions the miss. The campus pastor says: 'Good call on the allergy.' You nod. That's enough.

## C

The room absorbs this. Jackson's name does a lot of work. The campus pastor says: 'How'd it go today?' You say: 'It went.' He knows what 'it went' means. He writes something. You are not sure what. Jackson is coming back next week. That's the win and the miss.

## D:win

Gary looks at you. Gary is processing this. 'Kids ministry?' Gary asks. You nod. Gary looks at the middle distance. Gary is doing math. 'I did read to kids once,' Gary says. 'At the library.' He pauses. 'It went well.' The campus pastor looks at Gary. The campus pastor looks at you. Something has been set in motion that you did not fully intend.

## D:lose

Gary says: 'I actually have thought about it.' Pause. 'I have some curriculum ideas.' The campus pastor turns very slowly to look at Gary. Gary reaches into his jacket pocket. Gary has a printed page. Gary has come to this meeting prepared for a curriculum conversation. You look at the printed page. You cannot tell if this is the Westminster Shorter Catechism. You strongly suspect it might be the Westminster Shorter Catechism.

Around the room: Tech reports the stream was clean. Worship says second service had something. First Impressions says the door felt warm but the lobby felt cold. Nobody claims the lobby. Someone says the haze was a lot today. Nobody claims the haze.

'Same time next week.'

Four seconds.

Gary moves first.
