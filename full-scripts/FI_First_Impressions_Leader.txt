ROLE: First Impressions Leader (FI)
============================================================

------------------------------------------------------------
INTRO NARRATIVE
------------------------------------------------------------
Your alarm goes off at 6:30. Someone already made coffee. This is a good sign. You stand at the kitchen window for a moment with your mug. Today you will greet somewhere between four hundred and six hundred people, and every single one of them will form their first impression of Execution Church in the thirty seconds before they reach the auditorium. You know this. You take your job very seriously. You take it so seriously that the phrase "it's a door, {name}" has been said to you more than once in the past year.

You are short two greeters today. You found out last night. You've already filled one slot. The other is still open and you have been thinking about it since you woke up.

Welcome to Execution Church. Today is Sunday. This Sunday could make or break everything.
